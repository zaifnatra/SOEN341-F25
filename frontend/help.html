Need Help?

Frequently asked questions:

Account & Login:

  • If you are having issues logging in, make sure you are using your Concordia email.

Tickets & Events:

  • Claim a ticket under Events, and view it in My Events.
  • Paid tickets are added once payment has been safely and successfully completed.
  • If you don’t see your ticket, refresh the page or check under My Events.

For Organizers:

  • Create events under Account: Create Event.
  • View stats and export attendance in the Dashboard.
  • Generate a QR code to scan tickets at the entrance.

Troubleshooting:

  • Try refreshing or logging out and back in if pages don’t load.
